官宣! Fesod 成功进入 Apache 孵化器

2025年9月17日

Fesod 于2025年09月17日顺利通过投票，正式成为了全球最大的开源基金会 Apache 软件基金会 (ASF)的孵化项目。09月25日，Fesod 仓库正式迁移至 Apache 软件基金会。

Apache Fesod (Incubating) is an effort undergoing incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by the Apache Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
